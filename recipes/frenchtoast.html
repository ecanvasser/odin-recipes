French Toast Recipe

Description

One of my favorite breakfast meals growing up was French Toast. This sweet and simple breakfast delight will melt in your mouth with each bite and start your day off right. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • Brioche bread
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon sugar
  • Fresh fruit
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. 1. Crack your eggs into a mixing bowl and mix with cinammmon and vanilla extract.
  2. 2. Once you have your batter, dip the bread into the batter on both sides.
  3. 3. After coating the bread, place each slice onto a pan on low-medium heat.
  4. 4. Cook each side of the toast until it is golden brown.
  5. 5. Serve with fresh fruit and syrup...enjoy!
