BBQ Tri-tip Recipe

Description

If you're looking for a dish that reminds you of a traditional American summer, look no further. This simple and quick BBQ tri-tip recipe is the perfect fix for that memorable summer dinner you've been looking for.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tri-tip (any weight)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Remove the tri-tip from packaging and trim off any excess fat (unless it is pre-trimmed).
  2. Season very generously with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Place tri-tip on the BBQ when the grill has reached 400 degrees, and cook both sides for 10-15 minutes each.
  4. Remove tri-tip and let rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
  5. Enjoy!
