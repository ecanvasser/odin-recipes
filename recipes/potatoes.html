Pan-Fried Potatoes

Description

One of the most versatile side dishes you can make is pan-fried potatoes. Whether it's breakfast, lunch, or dinner, it pairs with nearly everything, and it's so simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Mini golden potatoes
  • Cast iron pan
  • Canola or Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Start by rinsing the potatoes and chopping into half-inch pieces.
  2. In a medium sized pot, place the potatoes in the water and bring to a strong boil.
  3. After reaching a boil, drop heat to a simmer and heat up the cast-iron with enough oil to coat the bottom of the pan.
  4. After the potatoes have simmered for 5-6 minutes, strain the water out over the sink. Make sure they are somewhat soft.
  5. Once potatoes are strained, fry in oil of choice until golden brown and season generously while cooking.
  6. Remove potatoes once they're golden brown and crispy, and enjoy!
